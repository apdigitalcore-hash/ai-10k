PRO TIPS FOR STRONGER CTAS:

1. One CTA per piece of content — don't give people 3 options.
2. Use urgency honestly: real deadlines close more than fake ones.
3. Micro-commitments work: "Comment YES" is easier than "Buy now."
4. The best CTA restates the benefit, then asks for the action.
5. Match the CTA energy to where the reader is: cold traffic needs
   low-commitment CTAs; warm audiences can handle a direct buy now.
